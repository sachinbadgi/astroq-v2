RESIDENTIAL HOUSE AS PER PLANETS 
Jupiterian House: An airy house not facing North-South. A 
courtyard on one side of the house and not in the centre. A peepal tree 
or a place of worship either be in the house or in close proximity. 
Solar House: An East facing house meant to receive sunlight. 
Courtyard in middle, water source on right side near exit gate and fire 
place in courtyard. 
Lunar House: A well, pond, flowing water inside/adjoining or very 
near the house. 
Venusian House: Opposite to Solar house. Entrance porch/entry 
gate facing North-South having white plaster. Some part of house would 
beKuchcha. 
Martian (positive) House: Mars moves along Sun, Jupiter and 
Moon are like sentry. A North-South house with entry gate Kuchha or 
pucca (cemented or uncemented). 
Martian (negative) House: A South facing house. Tree along 
with the house and shade of a tree falling on it. Sweetmeat shop or a 
furnace or funeral place nearby. Neighbours issueless, if not himself. 
Mercurial House: Vacant ground around of a lonely house. Trees 
with broad leaves. Jupiterean devoid of lunar trees around. If such trees, 
are there it will make Mercury inimical. (Peepal-Jupiter, Banyan- Jupiter-
Moon, Berry- Mercury) 
Saturnine House: A bounded house opposing to Sun and facing 
West. Dark room on right side, after entry. So long as there is no light 
in this room, Saturn gives benefic effect. Possibily a big stone in the 
house and entry steps are made of old wood. Perhaps having pillars too. 
Rahu House: A big covered earthen hole on right side while entering. 
Water exit at entry point of house. Opposite house is lying uninhabited 
or its owner is issueless. House roof has changed many a times. A 
furnace, source of smoke or dirty water pool in close proximity.
