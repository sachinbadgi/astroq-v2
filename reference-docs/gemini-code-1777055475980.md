# Lal Kitab Varshaphal: Advanced Conditional Rules
*Extracted from Lal Diary by Pt. Veni Madhav Goswami*

This document outlines the specialized conditional logic where the Birth Chart (Janam Kundli) positions interact with the Annual Chart (Varshphal) to trigger specific life events.

---

## 1. Financial & Prosperity Rules (Dhan/Labh)
Logic: `IF Janam_House == X AND Varshphal_House == Y THEN Result`

| Janam Position | Varshphal Position | Predicted Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Saturn 3 or 5** | **Ketu + Saturn / Rahu 1 or 3** | Wealth is acquired through intense self-effort. |
| **Ketu 11** | **Saturn 11** | Excellent period for financial growth and gains. |
| **Jupiter + Moon** | **Jupiter + Moon in House 9** | Acquisition of "Gupt Dhan" (Unexpected or hidden wealth). |
| **Jupiter + Moon 10** | **Jupiter + Moon 10** | **Danger:** Total destruction of wealth and assets. |
| **Saturn 6** | **Mars 1 to 8** | Sudden rise in fortune (Bhagyoday) and financial uplift. |
| **Mercury 3** | **Ketu 11** | Significant financial loss. |

## 2. Marriage & Relationship Timing (Vivah Yoga)
Specific timing constraints for marriage based on age and planetary placement.

* **The Age Gate:**
    * **Mercury (H 8, 9, 10, 12):** Parents will remain unhappy until the native is 25.
    * **Saturn (H 6, 7, 8):** Do not marry before age 28.
    * **Rahu (H 1 to 7):** Do not marry between ages 21 and 25.
* **Varshphal Trigger:**
    * If **Venus** moves to a house where it is helped by **Saturn** in the Varshphal, marriage is certain.
    * If **Sun + Moon** are in House 7 in the Varshphal, there is a danger of confinement (Jail) or mental distress.

## 3. Progeny & Children (Santan Yoga)
Rules determining the birth or health of children in a specific year.

| Janam Position | Varshphal Position | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mercury 12** | **Saturn 2** | If the native helps in the upbringing of others, their own wealth is destroyed. |
| **Jupiter 5** | **Venus 9** | Health of the native suffers; wealth from in-laws is lost. |
| **Mercury (Any)** | **Ketu in House 1** | Transfer in job OR the birth of a nephew/grandson. |
| **Moon** | **Venus** | Danger of "Sukhe ka Rog" (wasting disease) for a son or grandson. |

## 4. Travel & Foreign Movement (Yatra)
Rules extracted from the "Yatra" (Travel) sections of the diary.

* **Foreign Travel:** If **Mercury is in House 2** in the Janam Kundli and moves to **House 12** in the Varshphal, foreign travel is imminent.
* **Directional Travel:**
    * **North:** If House 6 has Mercury + Ketu OR Mars + Ketu.
    * **South:** If House 10-11 has Ketu + Sun OR Ketu + Mercury.
    * **East:** If House 1-5 has Ketu + Moon OR Ketu + Saturn.
    * **West:** If House 3 has Ketu + Jupiter.

---

## 5. Health & Disease (Rog) triggers
* **General Health:** If **Sun or Moon** meet **Mercury** in Houses 1, 6, 7, 8, or 10, health complications arise.
* **Father's Health:** If **Jupiter + Saturn** are in House 2 in both Janam and Varshphal, the father has a long life, but the native's own health remains poor.
* **Mother's Danger:** If **Mercury + Moon** are in House 11 in Janam, but Mercury moves to House 11 alone in Varshphal, it indicates a death-like struggle for the mother.

---

## 6. Real Estate & Construction (Makan)
* **Prohibition:** Do not build a house if **Saturn is in the 1st House** and **Houses 7 to 10** are empty.
* **Timing:** If building a house at age **36 or 39**, it may result in the loss of progeny.
* **Dispute:** Construction at age **55** leads to long-term illness.

---

## 7. Special Remedies for Saturn (Shani Upay)
If the Varshphal indicates a heavy Saturn affliction (Jail Yatra or loss of earnings):
1.  **Written Prayer:** Write "Ram Ram" with a red pen 21 times every Saturday and offer to Lord Hanuman.
2.  **The Flute Remedy:** Fill a flute with sugar (Khand) and bury it in a deserted location.
3.  **Kohl Remedy:** Bury 60g of black Surma (kohl) in a forest/deserted land.
4.  **Mustard Oil:** If Saturn is in the 4th House, do not offer mustard oil to the temple; instead, keep it in a dark corner of the house.
