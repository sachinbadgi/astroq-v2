# Lal Kitab Varshaphal Rules: Extraction from Pt. Veni Madhav Goswami's "Lal Diary"

This document contains a structured extraction of the conditional Varshaphal (Annual Chart) rules as presented in the provided images. These rules are categorized by their specific life impact and require a comparison between the Natal Chart (Janam) and the Annual Chart (Varshphal).

## 1. Financial & Prosperity Rules (Dhan/Labh)
These rules dictate the timing of wealth acquisition or financial setbacks.

| Janam House | Varshphal House | Outcome / Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Saturn (3 or 5)** | **Ketu + Saturn / Rahu (1 or 3)** | Accumulation of wealth through self-effort and hard work. |
| **Ketu (11)** | **Saturn (11)** | Exceptional financial gains and prosperity for the year. |
| **Jupiter + Moon** | **Jupiter + Moon in (9)** | Native receives "Gupt Dhan" (sudden or hidden wealth). |
| **Mercury (3)** | **Ketu (11)** | Significant financial loss (Dhan Haani). |
| **Saturn (6)** | **Mars (1 to 8)** | Sudden rise in fortune and overall wealth (Bhagyoday). |
| **Moon (10)** | **Saturn (1)** | Destruction of wealth due to bad reputation or social disgrace. |

## 4. Special Destruction Logic (Nisht Grah)
*From Page 200:*
* **The Sequential Impact Rule:** If a planet is in **House 8** in the Janam Kundli and moves to **House 7, 8, or 6** in the Varshphal, it systematically negates the positive effects of those houses in a chain reaction.

## 5. Remedial Protocols (Upay)
For heavy afflictions (particularly Saturn) identified in the Varshphal:
* **Ram Naam Scribbles:** Writing "Ram Ram" with a red pen on white paper 21 times every Saturday.
* **Flute Remedy:** Burying a flute filled with sugar (Khand) in a desolate or uninhabited area.
* **Kohl Burial:** Burying 60 grams of black surma (kohl) in forest land.
* **Milk Offering:** Pouring milk into a well or at the base of a Peepal tree to mitigate Saturn's severity.
